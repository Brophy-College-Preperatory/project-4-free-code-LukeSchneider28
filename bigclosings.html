Mr Beast Withdraws

Mr. Beast made his official withdrawl of Beast Burger when he made several tweets on the app Twitter about him no longer focusing on Beast Burger and moving into his next project, "Feastibles". Furthermore, Jimmy also sued the Beast Burger company because it was hurting his brand with its bad food quality. This marked the end of the era of Mr. Beast burger.
